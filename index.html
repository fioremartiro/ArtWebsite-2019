Fiorella Martiro

Biography


Fiorella Martiro was born in Cartago, Costa Rica on September 14, 1997. At the age of nine, she discovered her love for art and science that is how her passion for painting began. From the beginning of her career, she had great influences on artists, such as Vicent Van Gogh and Jean Michel Basquiat.

Work & Inspiration

Fiorella is an abstract painter. Her technique is acrylic on canvas; science is always presence in each one of her works as she is highly passionate about astronomy. Currently, Fiorella is developing as a “freelance” painter, making her paintings known through her Instagram account.
